About Henry

Henry Pandiella has been working in the building industry for over 35 years. He has been running his own business for 20 years, creating beautiful and unique outdoor spaces for a variety clients all over Sydney.

In his spare time he likes fishing, gold fossicking and collecting antique cameras and radios.

He is currently creating his own outdoor space with detailed mosaic work, as inspired by Gaudi.
